Why SignalWorks

Why I founded
Favatas SignalWorks

After 15 years inside some of the most data-rich organizations in financial services, media, and retail — I kept seeing the same problem: companies sitting on extraordinary data assets with no clear path from identity to impact.

The tools existed. The data existed. What was missing was someone who could sit at the intersection of strategy and execution — who understood the technical architecture AND the business outcome, and could translate between the two.

I founded Favatas SignalWorks LLC to be that bridge. Not as a large consulting firm with layers of account management between you and the person doing the thinking — but as a focused, senior-level engagement where you get direct access to someone who has actually built these systems, run these teams, and delivered these results.

The era of commerce media, clean rooms, and AI-driven identity is not coming — it's here. The organizations that move now will build compounding advantages that are genuinely hard to replicate. I want to help you be one of them.
